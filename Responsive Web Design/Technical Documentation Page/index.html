What is Teeworlds

Teeworlds is a free online multiplayer game, available for all major operating systems. Battle with up to 16 players in a variety of game modes, including Team Deathmatch and Capture The Flag. You can even design your own maps!

Installing Teeworlds

Go to ddnet.tw/downloads and download the latest version. Then set up your client by entering your nick and possibly clan. Check out different option tabs. If you can't find a server you are looking for, pay attention to your server filters. You can see which server filters are enabled on the right side of the screen when you select the "Filter" tab in the down right corner.

Playing with dummy

DDNet allows you to play with you dummy. You can play for two players this way, which is perfect considering... ehmm... you know. You can hammer fly with dummy and copy dummy so that he follows all your moves. Important thing to remember while playing with dummy is that if you hold a key down when switching to dummy the tee you switched from will hold this button down until you switch back. This is sometimes desired but sometimes not. You can change this behaviour with the following commands.

cl_dummy_resetonswitch 0

cl_dummy_resetonswitch 1
